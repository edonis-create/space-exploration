The Red Planet

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known for its reddish appearance.

Quick Facts

  • Diameter: 6,779 km
  • Moons: Phobos and Deimos
  • Orbit Period: 687 Earth days

Did You Know?

Mars has the tallest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.
