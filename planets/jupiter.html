The Giant Planet

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is known for its Great Red Spot.

Quick Facts

  • Diameter: 139,820 km
  • Moons: 79 known moons
  • Orbit Period: 12 Earth years

Did You Know?

Jupiter has the shortest day of all the planets, rotating once every 10 hours.
